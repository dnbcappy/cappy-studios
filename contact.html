Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use your games for content?

Yes! Feel free to stream, record, or create content around any of my games. A link back to Cappy Studios would be appreciated but not required.

Are your games free?

Currently all games are free to play. This may change for larger projects, but there will always be free content available.

How can I support Cappy Studios?

Play the games, share them with friends, leave feedback, and spread the word! Future support options like Ko-fi may be available later.

Are you open to hiring/freelance?

Currently focused on personal projects, but always open to interesting conversations. Reach out and let's talk!
