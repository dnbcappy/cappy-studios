About the Studio

Hey, I'm Dylan

Developer, game maker, and creative thinker based in Belgium. I left my job to go all-in on game dev and building things I care about. Cappy Studios is where those ideas become real projects.

It started with CS50 and a curiosity for how games work. That first project turned into Snake-50, and now there's a narrative adventure in development, idle games on the horizon, and always something new brewing.

"There is something to learn in every scenario."
