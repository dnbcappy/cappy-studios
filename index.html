The Vision

Every project starts with a spark of curiosity

Cappy Studios is a one-person creative studio focused on bringing ideas to life through games, web apps, and digital art. From retro-inspired web games to ambitious idle game projects, every creation is built with passion and a love for learning.

Based in Belgium, combining a background in finance with a deep passion for coding, game development, and creative expression. CS50 certified and always building something new.
