The Creator

Hey, I'm Dylan

Developer, game maker, and creative thinker based in Belgium.

By day, I work in billing and finance. By night (and every spare moment), I'm building games, learning new technologies, and bringing ideas to life. Cappy Studios is the home for all my creative projects - a space where "what if" becomes "let's build it."

The Story

It started with curiosity. What makes games tick? How do websites work? These questions led me to CS50, where I fell in love with programming and problem-solving. That first "Hello, World" turned into building real projects - Snake-50 was my first complete web game, featuring authentication, leaderboards, and multiple game modes.

Now, the vision is bigger. Idle games like Trucker Idle and Tea Farmer Idle are on the horizon. A narrative adventure called "Once I Was a Crybaby" is in development with a talented partner. And there's always something new brewing - whether it's a web app, a piece of pixel art, or an experiment that might become the next project.

There is value in every idea. That's not just a tagline - it's how I approach everything. Even the projects that don't ship teach something valuable. Every line of code is practice. Every failed experiment is a lesson.
